About

Joel Bijoy — based in Seattle, Washington. I’ve been making videos for years, starting with school projects, edits, and short films, but recently I’ve been taking it more seriously and building work for real people and businesses. I like making videos that bring color and capture the moment. Most of my work is short-form, promotional, and story-based. Whether it’s a barbershop, a sports edit, or a more personal project, I care about what the client wants.
